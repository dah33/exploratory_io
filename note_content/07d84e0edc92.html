My August Flight Delay Report

I have analyzed the airline delay data based on the latest data from the feaderal transportantion office. Overall, it is showing the trend from the last report with a few exceptions. 

When do the flights delay?

I have found that flights tend to delay at Friday and Sunday nights. This might reflect the fact that many business people travel around these times. 



Are Arrival Delay and Departure Delay times correlated?

The longer the delay times are the more correlation we can see. I have used K-means algorithms to cluster the delay data. The cluster 5 that is close to zero for both Arrival and Departure Delay times have less correlation between the two.
